JOBFLOW Automated Job Application &amp; Outreach Platform v3.0 — Complete Technical Specification Najd Salhi · 2025 · Written with all engineering constraints known from day one Executive Summary JobFlow is a self-hosted automated job application and outreach platform.
It discovers job listings across 5 EU countries daily, generates tailored CVs and cover letters, sequences cold email outreach, manages replies, tracks visa applications, and prepares for interviews.
The operator spends 30-45 minutes per day reviewing and approving AI-generated output.
Everything else runs autonomously.
WHO IT IS FOR A non-EU candidate applying to entry-level sales, customer success, and multilingual roles across Poland, Romania, Malta, Germany, and the Netherlands.
Needs to apply to 25-40 companies per day while working full time.
Cannot afford manual job hunting at that volume.

The decision of which tier to use is based on task complexity, latency requirements, and cost.
Simple classification tasks run locally or on Groq free tier.
Complex generation tasks run on Gemini 2.0 Flash.
Multi-step reasoning with tool use runs as Claude-style agents via the Gemini API.

EU law governing data collection, storage, and processing.

Supabase/PostgreSQL feature that restricts which rows users can see.
Every table has a policy: users can only see their own data.

30% of salary is tax-exempt for up to 5 years.
